Places to visit in Russia

Russia is on the bucket list of every traveller! The marvellous architecture, vast landscape, Slavic spirit and a proud nation are just a drop in the ocean of things Russia is famous for. The largest country in the world really has it all–mountains, valleys, frozen lands, and warm sands, and an incredible number of amazing natural destinations to impress any visitor. Some of the oldest cities in Russia, including Moscow and St. Petersburg, still retain their imperial splendor–obvious not only in their architecture but also in their majestic parks, shopping centers, and even metro stations.

Here are some great places to visit that can help you discover Russia as an ideal travel destination. In addition to this, browse more of this website for inspiration and information to plan your future trip so that you can discover the wonders of this fascinating country.

Ai-Petri Mountain, Crimea

This trip was thrilling because of the mountains and bridges between mountains. Ai-Petri is one of the symbols of Crimea and the main attraction in the area. One of the windiest peaks in Crimea is home to an incredible set of cable bridges. The mountain is located above the city of Alupka and has a height of 1234 meters (4049 ft.)Taking a cable car to the top of Mount Ai-Petri will be one of the most exhilarating experiences you will have while you visiting Crimea.

Sitting right on the Black Sea

Sochi

Sochi belongs to that tiny part of Russia, which is happily located in the subtropical climatic zone. This trip was a cool because of beaches. A summer beach resort town sitting right on the Black Sea, Sochi offers long stretches of pebble and sand beaches, imposing examples of Stalinist architecture, a summer film festival known as Kinotavr, and plenty of spas and outdoor markets to please all budgets and tastes.
